    about

    A little about me...

    I'm Emily Wright Moore — an illustrator and graphic designer based in Munich, Germany. I'm experienced in brainstorming, solving problems, and building and all kinds of strategic, saavy design both in print, on the web, or by hand. I focus on balancing usability and accessibility clearly and efficiently. I also really like what I do.

    I keep a blog for my illustration, design, and other creative pursuits: Scribbler. The blog focuses on web experimentation, storytelling, and showcasing the creative process while documenting my work, daily life, and personal practices. I also like to talk about bicycles, motorcycles, rock climbing, and tattoos.

    Raised in Texas, I studied at Parsons in NYC, and completed my BFA in Illustration at California College of Arts and Crafts in San Francisco.
